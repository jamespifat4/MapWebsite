Australian 2016 Census Analysis of Education and Training in NSW

Story: Education and training - Female - Cover’s 90% of NSW. - Huge escalations in educational and training occupations along the east coast of NSW whilst inland areas such as Wilcannia, Broken Hill, Swan Hill, Hay, Griffith, Warren, Parkes, West Wyalong is still present but not heavily. This reflects a lack of population growth compared to for example, Sydney's massive population splurge. - The space of land occupied by the this industry reflects the growth in population, both young and in job opportunity. Education - Male - Covers a small amount of NSW, primarily across the east coast, with only a few places inland. - These are: Balranald, Ivanhoe, Hay, Cobar, Boorke, West Wyalong and Brewarina. - Locations where Males work within education and training are all dominated by women when inland. It reflects that in the efforts of expanding NSW’s population out west is lead by the efforts of women in educating and training the youth and workers.
